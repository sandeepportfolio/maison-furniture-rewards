Full payment is due at the time of booking unless otherwise arranged. We reserve the right to cancel any reservation where payment cannot be processed or is suspected of being fraudulent.

3. Cancellation Policy

We follow a moderate cancellation policy:

  • Full refund if the reservation is cancelled at least 5 days before the scheduled check-in date.
  • 50% refund of the nightly rate if cancelled less than 5 days before check-in. Service fees are refunded in full.

Standard check-in time is 4:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM local time, unless otherwise communicated. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request but is not guaranteed. Guests who fail to check out by the designated time may be charged an additional night’s stay.

5. Age Requirement

All primary guests (the individual making the reservation) must be at least 21 years of age. Valid government-issued photo identification may be required at check-in. The primary guest is responsible for all members of their party and any visitors during the stay.

6. Guest Responsibilities & House Rules

Guests are expected to treat each property with care and respect. Specific house rules are provided for each property prior to check-in and must be followed throughout your stay. General responsibilities include:

  • No smoking of any kind inside the property.
  • No unauthorized parties or events. The number of overnight guests must not exceed the listed maximum occupancy.
  • Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM to respect the surrounding neighborhood.
  • Pets are only permitted at pet-friendly properties and must be disclosed at the time of booking.
  • Guests must secure the property (lock doors, close windows, set alarm if applicable) when leaving.

Violation of house rules may result in immediate termination of your stay without refund.

7. Property Damage

Guests are financially responsible for any damage to the property, furnishings, fixtures, or amenities caused during their stay beyond normal wear and tear. A damage assessment may be conducted after check-out. If damage is found, guests will be notified and charged for repair or replacement costs. We reserve the right to charge the payment method on file for damages up to the full replacement value of any damaged items.

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Booking details: check-in and check-out dates, number of guests, property preferences, and special requests.
  • Payment information: credit card details and billing address, processed securely through our third-party payment provider.
  • Identity verification: government-issued ID information when required for guest verification.
  • Communications: messages, inquiries, and correspondence you send to us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and manage your reservations and payments.
  • Communicate with you regarding your bookings, including confirmations, check-in instructions, and post-stay follow-ups.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve our properties, services, and website experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.
  • Send promotional communications about our properties and services, which you may opt out of at any time.

  • Payment processors: to securely handle payment transactions. We do not store your full payment details on our servers.

  • Communication tools: to send booking confirmations, reminders, and support responses.

These third parties are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Cookies & Tracking
