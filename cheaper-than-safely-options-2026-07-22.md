# Cheaper STR Damage Protection than Safely — Options (2026-07-22)

Requirements: host damage protection, ZERO guest contact for claims, DIRECT-bookings-only capable, cheap.

## ❌ Why not Safely
- Per-reservation cost is high; full $1M/$1M policy is host-absorbed and pricey. Sandeep rejected on price.

## ✅ #1 RECOMMENDATION: Waivo (waivo.io)
- **Coverage:** guest-caused damage $500–$20,000 (building + contents), incl. accidental, intentional,
  pet damage, and theft (w/ police report). NO deductible, first-dollar reimbursement.
  Separate **Waivo+ liability = $1,000,000** (can't be marked up). Base product is damage-only;
  for liability they themselves recommend Proper Insurance.
- **ZERO guest friction (verbatim):** "Waivo will never contact your guests or ask you to contact your
  guests at any point in the damage process. We have removed all guest communication." → strongest
  no-contact language of any provider. Claims = you submit damage notice + evidence to Waivo portal; they pay you.
- **CHEAP because guest-paid:** "most hosts use Waivo in lieu of a security deposit, so guests pay a small
  non-refundable fee." → effectively $0 cost to host. (Damage product markup-able; Waivo+ liability not.)
- **DIRECT-ONLY = trivial:** Waivo uses a client-portal / self-report ("upload monthly fee reports") model,
  so YOU choose which bookings to enroll → only report direct (Booking Engine) bookings. Inherently direct-only.
  No dependence on a provider channel filter (the exact trap that killed Truvi).
- **Pricing:** quote-based (Book a Consult / +1 855-270-9505). No public flat rate, but guest-paid structure
  makes it structurally cheaper than Safely for the host.
- **Fit:** serves Independent Hosts, Professional PMs, and Booking Platforms/OTAs (has API for platforms).

## ✅ #2 (FREE): Guesty native Security Deposit on the Booking Engine
- Set a refundable security-deposit / pre-auth hold on DIRECT (Booking Engine) reservations only. FREE, native.
- Direct-only by construction (configured on the booking-engine channel).
- Downside: NOT insurance — you claim against the card hold (can fail/expire), capped at hold amount,
  and MAY require charging the guest's card = some guest friction. Weakest on the "zero contact" goal.

## ⚠️ #3 Chargeautomation / Autohost (deposit + damage-waiver automation, Guesty-integrated)
- Automate a refundable deposit OR a guest-paid damage-waiver fee at direct checkout. Cheap, guest-paid.
- Deposit route can involve charging the guest (friction). Waiver-fee route can fund a real product.
- Worth a quote if Waivo's consult pricing disappoints.

## Honest notes
- $100M is NOT available as a per-booking/direct-only product (that's an annual umbrella via broker; covers
  the property for ALL guests regardless of channel). Liability high-limit = Proper Insurance annual policy.
- All "guest-paid" options cost the host ~$0; "cheapest" really = lowest guest-facing fee + real coverage.

## RECOMMENDATION
Lead with **Waivo** (zero guest contact — best in class, guest-paid = cheap, self-report = perfectly direct-only).
Keep the **native Guesty security deposit** as the free fallback. Get a Waivo quote at consult; if damage-only
isn't enough, pair Waivo (damage) + Proper Insurance (liability) — still cheaper/structurally cleaner than Safely.
